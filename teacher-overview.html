Responsibilities

The HHT Program is provided by part-time teachers who instruct students on an individual basis in a home school, home, hospital, or community setting. Teachers act as the liaison among students, parents, and home school in an effort to help students remain current with their regular classes.

Students typically receive six instructional hours a week on full-time HHT, unless otherwise recommended by the Home and Hospital teacher, IEP team or Problem Solving Team.
